E-Care

(E-Waste Club)

The E-Care Club, functioning under the PRISM Club, is dedicated to promoting responsible electronic waste management and environmental sustainability. The club aims to create awareness about the harmful impact of e-waste and encourage practices such as reduce, reuse, recycle, and safe disposal of electronic items. Through workshops, drives, and awareness campaigns, the E-Care Club empowers students to adopt eco-friendly habits and contribute towards a cleaner, greener, and more sustainable future.
